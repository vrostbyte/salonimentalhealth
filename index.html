About Megan

A partner in your
mental health journey

In Megan's Words

"Hi, I'm Megan. As a psychiatric nurse practitioner, I love helping people, especially when life feels heavy or overwhelming. My work isn't just about diagnoses or medication, but about understanding your story, what's getting in the way, and what helps you feel like yourself again. I take a down-to-earth, collaborative approach, and genuinely want you to feel heard and cared for. Together, we'll find a way forward that fits you and helps you reconnect with your best self."

My Approach

I combine medication management with psychotherapy in every session. My approach is collaborative, evidence-based, and patient-centered — creating a treatment plan that fits your unique story and goals. I believe care should be holistic, compassionate, and paced entirely at your comfort. Your goals are my goals.

What to Expect

Your first session will be an informal, relaxed conversation — all about you and how I can best support you. From there, we'll move forward with a thorough evaluation, regular follow-ups, and open communication so you always feel informed and comfortable with every aspect of your care.

Services

How I can help you

Evidence-based psychiatric care tailored to you — delivered virtually and on your schedule, including weekends.

🔍

Psychiatric Evaluation

Comprehensive initial assessments covering your history, current symptoms, and goals — the starting point for a care plan built just for you.

💊

Medication Management

Personalized, ongoing prescription oversight and careful adjustments to keep your treatment safe and effective at every stage.

🗣️

Supportive Therapy & Psychoeducation

Guided conversations, practical coping tools, and education to help you understand your experience and build lasting resilience.

👧

Children & Adolescents

Age-appropriate psychiatric care from toddlers through teens, with specialized focus on child mental health and developmental support.

🧬

GeneSight Testing offered

Pharmacogenomic testing that analyzes how your genes affect medication response — helping find the right medication faster.

🤝

Collaborative Care

Coordination with your therapists, primary care providers, and specialists so every part of your care stays connected and consistent.

Insurance & Billing

Transparent fees,
flexible options

Megan bills insurance directly when credentialed with your plan. If not credentialed, self-pay rates are available. Sliding scale eligibility is also available — because cost should never be a barrier to getting support.

  • Insurance billed directly when credentialed with your carrier
  • Competitive self-pay rates if not covered
  • Sliding scale — apply if you may be eligible
  • All major credit cards accepted (Visa, MC, Amex, Discover)
  • Weekend and flexible appointment times
  • Free 15-minute phone consultation before you commit
  • Ongoing messaging via Spruce between sessions
